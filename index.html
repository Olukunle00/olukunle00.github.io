About Me

A Visionary in AI, Education & Youth Empowerment

Author, speaker, mentor, and entrepreneur passionate about transforming lives through learning.

As the Head of Training at GMIND AI and Founder of The Ark Academy, I am deeply committed to leveraging Artificial Intelligence to revolutionize education and empower young minds. My journey involves both innovative program management in AI and hands-on mentorship, guiding teens and young people to discover their purpose and achieve their potential.

With over 5 years of experience in the education sector, including my role as a Teacher at Maravilla Schools, I bring a unique blend of pedagogical expertise and technological foresight. I've successfully engaged over 2000 teens and certified over 30 young individuals in online mentoring programs, demonstrating my dedication to impactful learning experiences.

My work extends to professional development as a Teachers Trainer and Online Teaching Transition Coach. I hold multiple certifications, including 'The Digital Teacher' from UNESCO MGIEP and 'Become an AI-Powered People Manager' from LinkedIn, reinforcing my commitment to continuous growth and cutting-edge educational practices.
