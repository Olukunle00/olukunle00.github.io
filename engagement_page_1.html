Workshop: Leveraging AI Tools for Classroom Innovation

At the National Teachers Conference, Olukunle led an interactive workshop designed to demystify AI for educators. The session provided hands-on experience with practical, free-to-use AI tools that can streamline administrative tasks, personalize student learning, and create dynamic lesson plans. The goal was to empower teachers with the confidence and skills to integrate technology seamlessly into their daily teaching, enhancing both their productivity and student engagement.

Workshop Highlights Included:

  • Live demonstrations of AI-powered lesson plan generators and grading tools.
  • Case studies on successful AI implementation in various subjects.
  • Practical exercises on creating interactive quizzes and educational games with AI.
  • A deep dive into best practices for classroom management in a tech-rich environment.
